Our Three-Step Process

  • Step 1: Talk to us
  • Step 2: Approve the design
  • Step 3: Go live

The first step is to talk to us.

Whether you have a specific theme in mind for your website, or you are open to suggestions, we can offer advice and ideas based on our years of experience. We are always happy to help with advice to find better solution. This consultation will clarify your business needs and hone in on the best design for your website. We can then agree on a design proposal moving forward.

LEARN MORE

Our expert designers will then create a modern, vibrant website to match your business needs and design brief.

You are then invited to view your new website design before it is made publically available on the internet. This gives you the opportunity to fine tune the design and content. Only after you are completely happy with your new website will we ask you to sign off on the website design and content, approving it to go live.

LEARN MORE

The final step in the process is the testing and live process.

We understand that the first impression a customer has of your business can define their view of your company. Before we put the site live and available to the public online, we need to be certain it is at the highest standard it can be. Your website will be rigorously tested to ensure that it works faultlessly, including proofreading and compatibility tests. Only when the website passes all our tests and checks successfully will it be uploaded and put live. This diligence and level of attention to detail is yet another reason to trust Wooweb’s design process.
